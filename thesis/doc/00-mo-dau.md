# Mo Dau

## Ly Do Chon De Tai

Canh long la mon the thao co lich su lau dai va phat trien manh me tren the gioi. Tai Viet Nam, canh long da tro thanh mot trong nhung mon the thao pho bien nhat, dac biet la trong nhan dan. Tuy nhien, tien do phat trien cua canh long Viet Nam con cham khi so sanh voi cac nuoc trong khu vuc va the gioi.

Hien nay, cac trung tam danh cho nhung nguoi hoc tap va tap luyen canh long van con cho nhieu han che:
- Website cua cac trung tam thong thuong la co co, khong phan hoa, kho su dung
- Khong co thong tin ro rang ve giao vien, lop hoc, va quy hoach tap luyen
- Kho ho tro khach hang trong phan dang ky, tuong tan hay lien he

De tro giup giai quyet nhung van de tren, em da chon de tai: "Thiet ke website gioi thieu trung tam the duc the thao canh long". Day la de tai thuc tien, co the cap dung vao thuc te, va giup nang cao hinh anh cua canh long Viet Nam.

## Muc Dich

Muc dich chinh cua do an nay la:

1. Xay dung mot website gioi thieu trung tam canh long modern, user-friendly, va responsive
2. Cap thong tin chi tiet ve canh long: lich su, loi ich suc khoe, cac doa tap tai trung tam
3. Giup khach hang (sinh vien, nguoi lon, ho khong) tim hieu ve cac lop hoc va dang ky tham gia
4. Nang cao nhan thuc ve canh long va su quan tam cua cong dong

## Doi Tuong Nghien Cuu

Website nay huong toi cac nhom doi tuong chinh:

- **Sinh vien, hoc sinh:** Muon hoc tap canh long, can thong tin ve lop hoc va doa tap
- **Nha quay quan:** Tim hieu ve canh long de phat trien la do the thao
- **Nha quan ly the thao:** Tim cach xay dung trung tam huong luyen chuan the gioi
- **Cong dong noi dai:** Muon suc khoe, co hoc tau canh long

## Pham Vi Do An

Pham vi cua do an nay bao gom:

- Thiet ke va xay dung website trang chu (landing page)
- Tao trang gioi thieu chi tiet ve trung tam va cac lop hoc
- Tao trang tin tuc, su kien cua trung tam
- Tao form lien he de khach hang co the gui thac mac
- Dam bao website responsive tren tat ca cac thiet bi (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Tieu chuan: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript vanilla, khong su dung framework phuc tap

## Phuong Phap Nghien Cuu

Em da su dung cac phuong phap sau:

- **Tham khao hoc thuat:** Nghien cuu cac bai bao, tai lieu ve web design, responsive design
- **Tham khao thuc te:** Tham khao cac website canh long chinh thuc cua cac nuoc (Anh, Nhat, Han Quoc)
- **Phan tich yeu cau:** Tham tam su dung de xac dinh tinh nang can thiet
- **Tham khao best practice:** Su dung cac chuan cua W3C, SEO, accessibility

## Cau Truc Do An

Do an gom 8 tuan phat trien:

- **Tuan 1:** Setup repository, phan tich yeu cau, ve ERD, xay dung skeleton
- **Tuan 2-4:** Code frontend (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
- **Tuan 5-7:** Viet quy nhan bao cao chi tiet
- **Tuan 8:** Finalize Docker, README, push GitHub, nop tran

Toan bo code se duoc luu tren GitHub va se co tag release tui tuan 8.
