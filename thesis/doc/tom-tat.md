## Abstract in English

Badminton is a promising sport in Vietnam, though badminton training centers still face challenges in management and online presence.

This thesis presents the design and development of a website introducing a badminton sports training center. The website includes:

- **4 main pages:** Home (index), About, News, Contact
- **Interactive features:** Responsive menu, image carousel, form validation
- **Responsive design:** Works on all devices (mobile 320px - desktop 1920px)
- **Technologies:** HTML5 semantic markup, CSS3 responsive, vanilla JavaScript, Express.js server, Docker containerization
- **Results:** Website tested on 5 browsers, WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance, LCP performance 1.8s

Development over 8-week sprint:
- Week 1: Setup & analysis
- Weeks 2-4: Frontend development
- Weeks 5-7: Thesis writing
- Week 8: Finalization & submission

The thesis has completed 100% of requirements, has potential for future backend expansion with database integration, and is ready for deployment and practical use.
